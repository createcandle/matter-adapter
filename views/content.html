You can try the steps again. Unplug it and click on the "It's unplugged" button. Wait a few seconds, then plug it in, wait a few more seconds, and click on the "It's plugged in" button.

How it works: the controller checks the list of devices that were plugged in before and after, and assumes that any new device that shows up in the second list must be the radio.

You can verify this yourself on the System Information page under Settings, where you can see a list of plugged in USB devices. Do you see some kind of 'serial' or 'UART' device there when it's plugged in?
